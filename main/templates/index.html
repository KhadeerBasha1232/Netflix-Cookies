Netflix Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device by websites. They serve various purposes, such as remembering your preferences, personalizing content, and analyzing site traffic.

In the context of Netflix, cookies play a crucial role in providing a seamless and personalized viewing experience. They help remember your login status, preferred language, and viewing history, allowing Netflix to tailor recommendations based on your interests.
